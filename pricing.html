Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work? ▼
You get full access to all content for 7 days. We'll send a reminder before your trial ends. Cancel anytime with one click - no questions asked. Your card won't be charged until after the trial period.
Can I switch plans later? ▼
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade to yearly, you'll receive a prorated credit for your remaining monthly subscription. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
Is there a refund policy? ▼
Yes. If you're not satisfied with your subscription, contact us within 30 days for a full refund. For yearly subscriptions, we offer prorated refunds within the first 60 days.
Can I share my account? ▼
Each subscription is for individual use only. However, we offer team and academy licensing for gyms and schools. Contact us for group pricing information.
How often is new content added? ▼
We add new lessons every week. Our instructors are constantly filming new techniques, competition breakdowns, and training concepts. Yearly subscribers also get access to exclusive monthly live seminars.
